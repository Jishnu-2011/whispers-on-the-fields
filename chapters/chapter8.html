Whispers on the Fields

Chapter 8

_______________________________THE LOSS THAT BURNED

The next match, I insisted on playing with full control—no blackouts, no ghost. Just me.

And I failed.

We faced Ryujin Academy. Ryujin was a force of chaos—his style was black lightning, unpredictable and destructive. He danced through our defense like a shadow with blades.

Raijin, in contrast, moved like light—effortless, graceful, pure. The ball obeyed him as if drawn to him by gravity.

Together, they tore us apart.

And then, the penalty. My chance to redeem myself.

But the ball slipped.

It was like my foot forgot the one thing it was born to do.

The silence after the miss was louder than any roar.

I collapsed to my knees.

And for the first time in my life, I wished I had never been born.

Chapter 53

_______________________________

After the crushing defeat against Ronaldo and Messi, Kamikaze and Zero found themselves at a crossroads. The loss was a bitter pill—but also a catalyst for change. They realized that if they wanted to survive and dominate in the Egoesta Champions League, they had to become more than rivals; they had to become teammates.

Zero extended a hand to Kamikaze, a silent truce forged in sweat and determination. They began training together every day—pushing each other to new limits. Zero, once labeled the worst player, now sharpened his defensive skills and tactical vision under Kamikaze’s fiery leadership. Kamikaze, with the monster inside him, learned patience and strategy from Zero’s calm precision.

Their synergy grew, blending Kamikaze’s explosive dribbling and raw power with Zero’s disciplined defense and game sense. Together, they became a force unlike any Egoesta had seen before—a perfect balance of light and darkness, chaos and control.

As the team solidified, their shared goal became clear: to climb the ranks, face tougher opponents, and claim their place among the true monsters of football.

The Egoesta Champions League had awakened a new Kamikaze—and now, with Zero by his side, the journey to greatness.

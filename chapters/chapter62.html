Chapter 62

_______________________________

The duel between Kamikaze and Noel Noa was unlike anything the football world had ever witnessed. Each move was lightning fast, a blend of precision, power, and unmatched creativity. Noel’s legendary skill pushed Kamikaze to his absolute limits, forcing him to tap into every ounce of his training — and the dark, fierce power of the Monster within.

Noel unleashed his signature dazzling dribbles and deadly shots, but Kamikaze’s instincts were sharper. Time after time, he blocked, dodged, and countered with breathtaking speed. The stadium erupted with every close call, every near goal.

Then, in a moment frozen in time, Kamikaze saw an opening — a sliver of space between Noel’s guard and the goal. With a burst of unstoppable energy, he weaved through Noel’s defense like a storm, the Monster’s aura roaring inside him. With one flawless strike, Kamikaze sent the ball soaring past Noel’s outstretched hands into the net.

The crowd exploded. Kamikaze stood tall, chest heaving, eyes blazing with triumph. Noel Noa, ever the sportsman, smiled and nodded in respect — the champion finally bested by the challenger who never gave up.

From that moment on, Kamikaze wasn’t just a player. He was a legend — the warrior who conquered the unbeatable, the one who proved that true strength comes from within.

Chapter 34

_______________________________

With Monster’s power unlocking his true potential, Kamikaze’s style transformed. No longer just a dribbler weaving through defenses, he became a lethal striker — the spearhead of Japan’s attack.

His shots burned with the fury of his spirit, precise and unstoppable. He hunted the goal like a predator, every touch purposeful, every strike a threat. The crowd began to whisper his name with awe — Kamikaze, the unstoppable striker.

But even as he scored goal after goal, the harsh truth remained: the Japan team was the worst in the tournament. Their defense crumbled, their passes faltered, and their cohesion was shattered. Kamikaze fought alone at the tip of an unraveling spear.

He became the team’s shining beacon — the striker who could pierce any defense, yet trapped in a squad that couldn’t hold the line or support his charge.

Every goal was a victory for himself, a solitary triumph amid a team sinking deeper.

Yet the fire inside him only blazed brighter. Kamikaze wasn’t just playing for goals anymore — he was fighting to carry an entire nation on his back.

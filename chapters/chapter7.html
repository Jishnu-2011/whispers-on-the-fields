Whispers on the Fields

Chapter 7

_______________________________THE FIRST VICTORY

Blood. Sweat. Screams.

We clawed our way to the regional finals and won. Barely.

I scored the winning goal in extra time—my mind blacked out again, the ghost inside me taking over.

When I came to, my teammates lifted me into the air.

“You did it!” they cheered.

But I didn’t smile.

Because it wasn’t me.

It was him—the other Kamikaze.

And deep down, that haunted me more than failure ever could.

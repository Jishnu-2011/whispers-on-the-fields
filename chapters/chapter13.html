THE UNDERGROUND GAME

One night, I was led underground—to a hidden facility beneath the stadium.

Jin called it the Zero Field.

It was where banned matches happened. Where monsters were trained in secret. Where the national team’s true formation was chosen—not by skill, but by survival.

I saw him there.

Zero.

A boy with no name. Masked. Silent. Unbeaten.

Even Raijin and Ryujin bowed their heads when he walked past.

“He’s the final striker,” Jin whispered. “If you survive long enough, you'll face him.”

I felt my blood freeze.

All this time, I thought I was fighting Ryujin and Raijin.

But they were the warm-up.

Zero was the final war.

And the thing inside me?

It wanted to meet him.

Not to win.

But to destroy him.

And take his place.

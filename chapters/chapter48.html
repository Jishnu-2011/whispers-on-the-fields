Chapter 48

_______________________________

Jin chuckled softly, shaking his head as he looked at Kamikaze.

“This World Cup? It’s nothing but a playground for beginners, amateurs. The real stage—the true warriors—are out there, in leagues you’ve never even heard of. Some of them have retired from the spotlight, but their skills? Untouchable.”

He paused, letting the weight of his words sink in.

“You think this was hard? That was just the first step, a test for rookies. The battles that really matter are in the shadows—the underground tournaments, the secret leagues where the best players who ever lived still compete.”

Kamikaze’s eyes widened, a new fire igniting inside him.

“So, you’re saying... the real challenge is still ahead?”

Jin nodded, his expression serious.

“Exactly. And if you want to be more than just a flash in the pan, you’ll need to push beyond everything you know. That’s where legends are made—and where monsters like you will be truly tested.”

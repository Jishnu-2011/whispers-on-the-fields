Chapter 19

The ninja

But far from it all, on a lone rooftop, one figure watches silently.

His black boots rest on the edge.

Long coat sways in the Tokyo night breeze.

A lone portable holo-screen floats in front of him, streaming the game live from a drone’s-eye view.

Zero.

Not in the stadium.

Not in the team.

Not in Japan’s future plans.

But watching.

Studying.

Calculating.

Voice from his comms:

“They finally put in Kamikaze. 83rd minute.”

Zero’s lips twitch—not a smile, just tension.

“Took them long enough,” he mutters.

“The monster’s finally waking up.”

A silent beep. Data flows across the side of the screen—heart rate, oxygen levels, sprint output. Kamikaze’s body, breaking human limits.

Zero’s eyes narrow.

“But it won’t be enough.”

Behind him, a team of Germany’s analysts monitor the feed.

“Shall we prep defensive counters for Ryujin and Raijin?”

Zero answers coldly:

“Forget them.”

“They’re relics. Flashy shadows.”

He leans forward, watching as Kamikaze breaks two defenders in one impossible feint.

“That one—Kamikaze... is reality.

He bleeds. He breaks. But he never stops.”

“He’s not a striker.”

“He’s a storm.”

"He is the puppeteer himself"

Final shout of the rooftop:

"I’ll meet you in the final, Kamikaze..."

"...and I will erase you."

This was a unlikely thing to do by zero

💀 WHAT MAKES ZERO DIFFERENT?

1. No Emotion

Zero doesn’t celebrate goals. Doesn’t shout. Doesn’t flinch.

His play is cold, clinical, terrifying.

He doesn’t win with power or chaos—he wins with mathematical inevitability.

Every move is pre-calculated. Every touch drains hope.

2. The Anti-Kamikaze

Kamikaze plays with heart → Zero plays with silence

Kamikaze is raw, impulsive, emotional → Zero is pure logic

Kamikaze wants to prove his worth → Zero exists as if it’s already proven

3. Mirror of the Past

Some say Zero used to be like Kamikaze—fiery, chaotic, human.

Until Jin broke him down.

Erased his identity.

Rebuilt him as the perfect machine.

He is called “Zero” because that’s what remains when you strip away ego, emotion, identity.

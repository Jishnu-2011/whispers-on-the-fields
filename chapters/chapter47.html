Chapter 47

_______________________________

Jin’s voice dropped to a whisper, leaning closer to Kamikaze as the crowd’s roar faded into the background.

“This has been a secret, Kami… but you still haven’t seen the outside world. The real battles, the true scale of what’s out there — it’s far beyond what you’ve faced so far.”

Kamikaze’s brow furrowed, a mix of curiosity and caution flickering in his eyes.

“The World Cup,” Jin continued, “it’s only the beginning. Beyond this stadium, beyond these matches, lies a world of challenges that will push you to your absolute limits.”

A chill ran down Kamikaze’s spine. The path ahead wasn’t just about football — it was about survival, growth, and facing shadows he hadn’t yet imagined.

He clenched his fists, heart pounding with anticipation. “Then I’ll prepare for everything. No matter how big the world is, I’ll carve my place in it.”

Jin smiled, a knowing glint in his eye. “Good. Because that’s what it takes to become a legend.”

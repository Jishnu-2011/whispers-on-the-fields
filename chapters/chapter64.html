Chapter 64

In the stillness of countless silent battles, Kamikaze transformed. Each day under Jin’s relentless guidance honed his skills sharper than ever before. No crowd to witness, no praise to soften his resolve — only the cold, focused grind of becoming a force unlike any other.

Word began to spread quietly through the elite football circles: Kamikaze was no longer just a player. He was the shadow stalking Noel Noa, the phantom rival haunting his every move. Every tactic, every strike, every dribble was a calculated nightmare designed to unravel the world champion’s perfect game.

Noel Noa, once untouchable, now found himself unsettled, his confidence chipped away by the specter of Kamikaze — the relentless storm that refused to relent.

The stage was set for their inevitable clash, a battle not for glory, but for survival in the cruelest, quietest war football had ever seen.

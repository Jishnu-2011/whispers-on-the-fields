Chapter 54

_______________________________

For the next year, Kamikaze and Zero pushed themselves harder than ever before. Every single day, from dawn till dusk, their training was relentless—drills, sprints, tactical sessions, mental conditioning. No rest. No excuses.

Their eyes were set on the ultimate goal: to finally defeat the legends Messi and Ronaldo, the only opponents they still feared and respected. Kamikaze’s monster grew stronger, more controlled, and Zero’s defensive mastery became nearly impenetrable.

Together, they refined their skills to near perfection. Kamikaze’s dribbling became a hypnotic dance; Zero’s positioning and anticipation turned him into a wall no attacker could breach.

They weren’t just training to win games—they were training to conquer giants. The world’s best awaited, but Kamikaze and Zero were determined to rise above.

A whole year of sweat, pain, and sacrifice forged them into warriors ready to face Messi, Ronaldo—and anything the world could throw at them.

Whispers on the Fields

Chapter 30

_______________________________

Kamikaze lay on the scorched earth, chest heaving, each breath a battle against an invisible enemy squeezing the life from him.

His vision dimmed, the world fading into a cold abyss.

The roaring silence inside his head was louder than any stadium cheer.

He was on the edge — a thin line between life and oblivion.

Memories flashed—his mother’s voice, Jin’s steely gaze, the shattered dreams, the burning desire to win.

But now, in this cruel moment, all those dreams seemed so fragile, so distant.

He wanted to fight—he had to fight.

But his body betrayed him, slipping into darkness.

THE SHADOW ABOVE THE STADIUM

Somewhere above the roaring chaos of the Japan National Stadium…

The match is fire.

Kamikaze just stepped on as a substitute.

Ryujin and Raijin are already dancing—dark matter and lightning, breaking ankles, tearing lines.

Commentators scream.

Fans chant.

And everything shifts.

Not immediately. Not magically.

But violently.

He shoves past Ryujin.

He ignores Raijin’s elegant feints.

And when Zero scans him—

—he moves in ways even the AI can’t read.

He scores once.

Then twice.

3–2.

The stadium erupts.

But Kamikaze doesn't celebrate.

He glares at the bench—

—at the system that sat him out.

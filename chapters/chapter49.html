The air grew heavy as Jin led Kamikaze to a secluded rooftop under the shimmering city lights. From the shadows, a figure emerged—tall, calm, and radiating an almost otherworldly aura.

Noel Noa stood there, his eyes sharp yet serene, the very embodiment of a world champion’s poise. His presence was magnetic, and even Kamikaze’s monster within felt a ripple of unease.

“This is him,” Jin said softly. “The real Noel Noa. The one who’s been watching, waiting. The one you’ll have to surpass if you want to claim your place among the legends.”

Noel gave Kamikaze a slow nod, a faint smile curling at his lips — a silent challenge.

Kamikaze’s heart pounded fiercely. This was no longer just a dream or a whispered legend. This was the moment everything he’d trained for converged: face to face with the ultimate rival.

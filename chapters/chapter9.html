THE REDEMPTION PROMISE

I sat alone that night under the rusted goalpost of our school’s forgotten training ground.

Rain soaked me to the bone.

I didn’t move.

Didn’t cry.

Didn’t speak.

Until I whispered:

“I will destroy them. I don’t care if it kills me.”

From that day on, I trained with one thought—rage.

I didn’t want to be like Raijin.

I didn’t want to be like Ryujin.

I wanted to become something else.

Something they couldn’t predict.

Something born from pain, from loss, from hate.

I would become the storm between light and shadow.

I would become Kamikaze.

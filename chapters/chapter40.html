Match 5: Japan (30) vs Qatar (15)

The game ended in a brutal 2-2 draw after 90 minutes of relentless battle. Both teams fought tooth and nail, neither willing to back down.

The tension skyrocketed as the match went to penalties.

Qatar’s first kicker stepped up — goal.

Japan’s first penalty was Kamikaze’s. His heartbeat thundered, but he struck the ball cleanly, top corner — goal.

Penalty shootout score: 1-1.

Qatar scored their next two penalties with deadly precision.

Japan matched them, but it was nerve-wracking.

The shootout stretched on, 6-6 after 12 shots.

The 13th penalty was Kamikaze’s again.

With the Monster’s fierce spirit raging inside, Kamikaze ran up, eyes blazing.

He struck with raw power and perfect aim.

Goal.

Qatar’s final penalty taker shot — saved by Japan’s keeper.

The stadium exploded.

Japan wins the penalty shootout 7-6.

Kamikaze’s teammates rushed him, lifting him up.

GHOSTED

Kamikaze watches from the sidelines.

Commentators mock:

“From national headlines to a benchwarmer… maybe Kamikaze was a fluke.”

The coach avoids his eyes.

His old teammates (Raijin and Ryujin) don’t even glance his way.

The whistle blows.

And in just 18 minutes, Heaven XI scores 3 goals.

THE CALL TO THE BIG LEAGUE

Weeks passed. Then one morning, a white envelope lay on my windowsill.

No return address. Just a red seal with the crest of the Japan National Youth Team.

My hands shook as I opened it.

“You have been selected.”

I dropped to my knees.

It was real.

This was my war now—not just to prove I belonged.

But to end them.

Ryujin. Raijin.

The twin titans stood atop the football world like gods.

But even gods fall.

And I was coming.

Chapter 6

_______________________________ THE RIVALRY

Then there was Riku. A shadow in my path. His eyes always on me—cold, calculating, silent. He never spoke much, but when he did, his words pierced like knives.

“You think you can lead this team?” he scoffed one night after practice, his voice low enough for only me to hear. “You’re not even a real striker.”

I didn’t reply. I didn’t need to.

Because every time we stepped onto the field, the real war began.

We weren’t teammates—we were predators sharing the same hunting ground. Every sprint was a race. Every shot was a challenge.

And then one day, he disappeared.

They said he transferred to the Raijin Academy—home of the twin gods of Japanese football: Raijin and Ryujin.

That’s when I knew.

I wasn’t just fighting Riku anymore. I was chasing ghosts. Legends.
